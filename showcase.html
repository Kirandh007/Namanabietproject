Our Story

Built for campus moments that feel special.

Namana & Preethi's Lounge brings the polish of an international dining room into the heart of the Data Science Department at BIET College, Davangere. It is imagined as a place where students, faculty, friends, and families can slow down, share comforting food, and turn everyday meals into memorable rituals.

Our vision is simple: serve familiar Indian favorites with premium presentation, consistent warmth, and a calm atmosphere that makes every guest feel welcomed. Every dish is selected for comfort, freshness, and the joy of eating together.
